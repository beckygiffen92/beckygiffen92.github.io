about
____

Hello I'm Rebecca and I am a BA Internet Design Graduate, nice to meet you! My journey in the industry started many years ago after completing a Foundation Year in Computing, however I found it a bit 'program heavy' which is when I developed more of an interest in designing instead. I have always been interested in art; from the age of 3, I fondly remember being happiest with a pile of A4 paper and pencils - usually developing pre-school peers caricatures and drawing animals. My family took me to a Saturday class at local art gallery at a young age, so creativity has always been a part of me.
